# Governance Analysis: Business Impact

## Outcomes
This governance framework improves security posture and operational control without requiring application changes.

## Business Value

### Audit Readiness
- Continuous compliance visibility
- Evidence trail stored via AWS Config delivery channel

### Cost Visibility
- Tag enforcement improves cost allocation by CostCenter and ownership reporting

### Operational Accountability
- Owner and Name tagging enables faster escalation routing and incident ownership

### Resilience
- S3 versioning improves recovery capability for operational mistakes and rollback scenarios

## Executive Summary
By enforcing encryption, tagging, and versioning baselines, this framework reduces preventable risk and improves governance maturity in environments where teams provision resources frequently.
